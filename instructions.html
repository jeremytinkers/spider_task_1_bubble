INSTRUCTIONS

1. Bubbles will randomly be generated with different colours and sizes.

2. The goal of the game is to click and eliminate all the bubbles.

3. But note : A 10 second timer will start when the bubbles cover 75% or more of the game arena

4. If you manage to decimate the bubbles in time, the timer will restart and you're good to go!

5. A pause and resume button have been placed and can be used.

6. So, Go! Have fun!
